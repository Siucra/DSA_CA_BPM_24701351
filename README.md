DATA STRUCTURE & ALGORITHM CONTINUOUS ASSESSMENT - BIKE PARKING MANAGER (24701351)
- The theme I chose for this continuous assessment is a Bike Parking Manager which falls under the Mobility Innovation theme of the Smart Docklands pilot call.
- The goal of this application is to manage bike parking spaces efficiently in the Docklands area while encouraging sustainable and active travel.
- As Dublin continues to promote cycling as a greener alternative to cars, one of the common challenges cyclists face is finding a secure and available place to park their bikes. 
- The Bike Parking Manager aims to address this problem by providing a simple system that tracks available bike parking spaces and manages demand when those spaces are full.
